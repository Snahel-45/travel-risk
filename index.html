About Us

Adventure Thrills in Rishikesh is your one-stop destination for adrenaline-pumping outdoor experiences in the heart of the Himalayas. We specialize in Paragliding, River Rafting, Bungee Jumping, Camping, and Bike Rentals in Rishikesh.

Founded by a passionate team of adventure enthusiasts and certified professionals, our organization brings together experts from the fields of outdoor sports, safety, travel, hospitality, and adventure tourism.

Our crew includes experienced instructors, safety marshals, and local guides who are committed to delivering memorable and secure adventure experiences.
